01. Fuel Cell Technologies

A fuel cell works much like an electric battery, converting chemical energy into electrical energy using the movement of charged hydrogen ions across an electrolyte membrane to generate current. It uses Hydrogen as fuel which is also considered as the ‘fuel of the future’.

We offer advisory services for fuel cell components and systems, fuel cell development, marketability and application expertise.


02. Electric Vehicles

Electric Vehicles(EV) are regular vehicles with their combustion engines replaced by an electric motor. EVs are considered to be the future of transportation and every automobile company is shifting its focus from combustion vehicle to electric vehicles.

We provide well-researched advisory services, actionable insights, policy and regulation understanding and perspectives in e-mobility for strategic decision making.


03. Waste Management

Smart waste management technology enables businesses to cut costs, reduce their business's environmental impact and convert their waste products either into energy or other products that can be marketed for profits.

We provide technology acquisition, government policy insight, auditing services and logistics related to waste handling, segregation and recovery towards achieving a sustainable goal for your business.


04. Biomass

Biomass technologies decompose organic matters to release their stored energy such as biofuels and bioenergy. Biomass energy is generated from either the combustion of biomass or the anaerobic gas from biomass.

We provide market analysis and research, feasibility studies, business planning and logistics and policy and regulations understanding throughout the project development and implementation.


05. Energy Storage

Energy storage technology captures energy and effectively deliver it for future use. There are several methods for storing energy such as mechanical, electrical, chemical, electrochemical, and thermal.

We provide consultancy, financial analysis and procurement of independent energy storage and hybrid renewable energy storage projects.


06. Solar (Rooftop and Ground Mounted)

Solar technologies convert sunlight into electrical energy either through photovoltaic (PV) panels or through mirrors that concentrate solar radiation. Energy harnessed is free of cost and do not require any moving parts.

We provide all services from the project’s inception to its completion including certifications, design, procurement to final plant handover.


07. Wind Energy

Wind power is one of the fastest-growing renewable energy technologies. The wind is used to produce electricity using the kinetic energy created by air in motion. This is transformed into electrical energy using wind turbines or wind energy conversion systems.

We provide consultancy, market research, procurement and consultancy services for wind power systems and components for your business.
